Library View

Hit the Refresh button to update/import EPUB 3 Audio-eBooks into your library. ()

You can sort your books by Title or by Author. ()

You can search for a book using the search bar at the top of the screen.

Tap on a book to open it.

Tap on the right icon of a book to open its metadata. ()

You can modify the global preferences of the app in the Options page. ()

 

Reader View

Chapters with an associated audio track show the track number in the title header (e.g., [1]).

Tap on the top left icon to go back to the library View. ()

Tap on the title header to show/hide the bottom bar.

Tap on the top right icon to open the preferences. ()

Tap on a text fragment to play the audio from the beginning of that fragment.

Tap on the active fragment to pause. Tap again to resume.

Tap on the central button of the navigation bar to show/hide more options.

Swipe left/right to go to the next/previous chapter.

Tap on the left/right blank margins to scroll up/down the text. (You need to enable this feature in the Options.)

Privacy Policy

We value your privacy as much as we value ours. ReadBeyond does not collect any data about your eBooks, reading preferences, or reading statistics. On Android, the Network connection permission is requested solely because Minstrel uses a WebView ("in-app browser") to render the eBooks. The app does not send data to nor receive data from the Internet, except for downloading a file when the user selects "Open with Minstrel" in the browser.
